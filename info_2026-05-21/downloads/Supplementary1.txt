THIoTwLEdoes VIO work?

SUBTITLE

· Visual inertia odometry (VIO) estimates
    drone position and orientation by
    combining camera and IMU data.

· OpenVINS is used to perform the VIO
    function.

· The vio_node feeds camera data and IMU
    data to OpenVINS.

· vio_node grabs camera data from gazebo
    camera (if x500 virtual drone), or
    Realsense (if physical drone).

· vio_bridge grabs imu from gazebo or PX4
    flight controller directly.

TWIThaLtEis meant by camera data?

SUBTITLE

· VIO is only interested on features in the image
    not everything in the image.

· Features are some distinct visual points in the
    image ­ sharp changes in brightness, corners
    and edges.
      · Plain feature environment might not be so
          good.

· Some popular feature extraction algorithms
    are SIFT, ORB, Harris, Shi-Tomasi. Opencv has
    libraries to do these algorithm.

fSUeBTaITLtEure?

· VIO algorithm matches and track
     features across frames to analyse
     how those features move.

· Sparse Optical Flow algorithms like
     Lucas-Kanade method (LK)
      · A popular local method that
           solves the brightness constraint
           over a small 3x3 or 5x5 window
           around a pixel, assuming
           constant motion in that
           neighborhood. It is fast and
           suitable for real-time tracking
           (often used with Shi-Tomasi
           corner detection)

· OpenCV has libraries that does optical
     flow calculation.

· In other words, movement is

     important. Movement of camera is

     especially needed during

     initialization to trigger calculation.

     Often we move the drone with the

     camera by hand.                         6

ITMITULdEata and Pose Estimation

S·UBTIATL3ED object is represented onto the Image Plane (2D image)
      using Extrinsic Matrix and Intrinsic Matrix:
        · Extrinsic Matrix: It is external to the camera describing
              transformation with the World and camera.
        · Intrinsic Matrix: It is internal to the camera describing
              transformation from camera coordinate to image plane.

· IMU data is fused with position estimation to determine the
      position of the camera based on the movement.
        · IMU data needs to transform to camera frame and from
              camera frame back using extrinsic matrix, to fuse with
              position and movement estimation.

· Intrinsic Matrix is used for epipolar geometry calculation.

· OpenVINS and other VIO algorithms need to be provided with
      values for extrinsic matrix (transformation between IMU and
      camera) and intrinsic matrix.

· IMU-Camera calibration needs to be performed to determine
      these matrix value.
        · Calibration uses a 8 x 8 April tag board as the known
              distance for the software to determine the values.
        · Refer to https://docs.openvins.com/gs-calibration.html
              if you like to know how it is being done.
        · You do not need to perform this calibration process for
              the Final Challenge drone. But need to be aware that
              placement of camera on the drone must not be
              changed as it will invalidate the calibration.

THIoTwLEdoes PX4 uses VIO?

SUBTITLE

· PX4 can use VIO instead of GPS as a source of
      · Horizontal position
      · Vertical position
      · Velocity

· You need to configure it.

· PX4 uses EKF2 to fuse the inputs with IMU
    data and barometer data, to provide the final
    position estimation.

· During startup, PX4 will wait for position
    estimate from VIO (vio-node). If not received,
    EKF2 will not be ready and PX4 unable to arm.

· As such, it is important to make sure vio-node
    is running and physically take up the drone to
    do slight movement along all axis of the
    drone to trigger VIO initialization. Perform
    until the initialization is successful.

· When initialization is successful, vio-node will

     output position estimate.                      9

THIoTwLEto assess VIO position is ok?

SUBTITLE

· Observe the vio-node position estimate:
      · If the position deviates too much from
           ground truth when you move, bad
           calibration.
            · Solution: Restart and redo.
      · If the position estimate drift infinitely, it is
           no good due to bad physical initialization.
           Wrong extrinsic and intrinsic.
            · Solution: Recalibrate.
      · If stuck at waiting for imu data, imu
           source not ready.
            · Solution: Check if wire between flight
                 controller and offboard is intact.
