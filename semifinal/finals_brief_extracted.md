## Slide 17

- Testing Rules
- Mapping Drone Cage (University only)
- Each Team will be entitled to 5 mins to testing in the Drone Cage.
- Hula Drone Cage (University & Pre-University)
- At most 2 teams can test in 1 cage at any point of time.
- There will be no limit to the total amount of time for testing.
- However, each testing session will be limited to 5 mins.
- There will be a cooldown of 20 mins after each testing (No re-queue for 20mins).
- Other rules
- There will be drones provided for testing at your tables. Strictly
- no flying
- is allowed outside of the cage.

## Slide 18

- Detailed Regulations
- Testing operates on a First-Come-First-Serve (FCFS) queue system. No prior online booking of testing slots is required.
- Teams may only join the queue when they are ready to test. When one team is testing, the next 2 team in the queue will be notified to report to the waiting area to prepare the drone.
- Teams next in queue who do not report to the waiting area will be removed from the queue.
- The testing timer starts when the team takes possession of the testing area and is cleared by the marshal to commence testing (i.e. drone take off); and ends when the drone lands.
- The organiser reserves the right to adjust queue operations and utilise unused testing capacity to maximise the overall participation and testing opportunities.
- Throughout the challenge, teams will not be given re-assessment attempts should the drone crash due to any reasons.
- Teams who violate the testing regulations or ignore instructions provided by test marshals will
- face 1h no-testing penalty
- .
- For mapping drone only
- For the mapping drone, each Team is allocated a fixed amount of time for testing per day to ensure fair access to the testing facilities.
- Teams may end a testing session before fully utilising their allocated testing allowance.
- Any unused timing remains available to the team and may be utilised in subsequent testing sessions.
- Teams with remaining testing allowance may re-join the back of the queue.
- Unused testing allowance is non-transferable between teams.
- No additional testing time will be granted if a team is unable to complete its planned activities within the testing allowance.
